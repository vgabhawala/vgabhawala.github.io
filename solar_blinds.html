Solar Powered Blinds Operator

Project Timeline: October 2023 – December 2023

The project is a dual-mode automatic and manual window blind operator. A photoresistor provides automated functionality: when a light-intensity threshold is surpassed, the servo automatically actuates the blinds. The blinds can also be controlled manually via an IR remote.

The IR remote includes buttons for three preset angles, a power button to toggle auto/manual modes, and arrows that adjust angle in 10° steps. An Arduino controls the photoresistor, IR receiver, and SG90 servo.

Components

  • 1× Solar Panel
  • 1× Voltage Regulator
  • 1× Arduino Uno
  • 1× SG90 Servo Motor
  • 1× IR Receiver
  • 1× Photoresistor

Final Design

A 3D-printed casing houses the Arduino, IR receiver, and supporting circuitry. A custom servo attachment securely couples the servomotor to the blinds for smooth, consistent operation.
